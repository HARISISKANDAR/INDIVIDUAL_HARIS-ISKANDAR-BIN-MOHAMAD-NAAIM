Let's listen!



Hi my name is Haris iskandar Bin Mohamad Naaim. This time, I will tell you a little bit about my experience during my stay at UiTM Kedah branch.


In the beginning, I entered this Institution of Study after finishing my studies in high school and with quite satisfactory SPM results. On September 2019 I was accepted at UiTM Kedah in the field of Diploma in Information Management.

In semester 1, my classmates and I were already together celebrating a rather sad day when one of our lecturers will move to another campus.


'Lecturer transfer'


Not only that, during my time here, there was also a bitter experience when me and a group friend were scolded by a lecturer for being late to class which we were expelled after entering the class, On that day also we had to present the diorama project we had worked on for 3 consecutive days.

By 2020, the country was shocked by the outbreak of Covid-19 which forced all sectors in the country to close and including educational institutions which forced us to return to our respective homes. On that basis, my experience on campus has stopped there and until now my friends and I have not been able to go through the learning as usual and we even had to go through the learning through online which gives us new challenges.


'Online Distance Learning'


Finally, I just want to say that I am very grateful to be able to gain knowledge in the making of this website which gives new experience and knowledge and once I would like to thank all the teaching staff for trying to teach me and other students without know the meaning of tired.

THANK YOU!
